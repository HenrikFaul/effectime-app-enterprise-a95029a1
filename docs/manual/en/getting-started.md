# Getting started

Effectime is a multi-workspace enterprise platform for workforce time, leave, capacity, organization, and approval orchestration.

## Header anatomy

After signing in you land on the **Workspaces** screen. The header includes:

- **Help (?)** on the **left** — opens a context-aware drawer for the page you are currently on.
- **Workspace switcher / actions** in the centre.
- **Language selector** (circular flag) on the right — change language at any time; your choice is persisted on your profile.
- **Sign out** on the far right.

Inside a workspace, the same Help and Language controls are available, plus tabs for Members, Organization, Calendar, Requests, Resources, Rules, Notifications, Reports & Audit, and Settings.

## Roles at a glance

- **Owner** — full administrative control of the workspace.
- **Resource Assistant** — manages members, allocations, rules, integrations, reports.
- **Member** — submits leave requests, sees their own calendar and team coverage.

## First steps

1. Create or accept a workspace.
2. In **People → Organization**, set up your structure, leadership levels, and contract types (use **Seed defaults** for a quick start).
3. In **People → Members**, invite your first colleagues.
4. In **Settings → Localization**, review the workspace language defaults.
