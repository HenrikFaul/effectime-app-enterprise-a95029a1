# Members

The Members tab lists everyone in the workspace. Owners and Resource Assistants can:

- **Invite** a new member from the header.
- **Open a profile sheet** to view a member's organizational metadata, allocations, leave history, peers per role, and notification preferences.
- **Edit** name, position, allocations, office, city, and base working hours.

## Organization completion banner

When a member is missing one or more of the new organization fields (manager, organizational unit, contract type, leadership level), a yellow banner appears at the top of the profile sheet listing the missing fields. The banner is advisory — existing data is preserved.

## Inviting a member

The invite dialog includes:

1. **Identity** — name + email.
2. **Permission role** — Owner / Resource Assistant / Member.
3. **Position(s)** — either a free-text label, or pick from the **predefined position catalog** (recommended skills are reviewed and selectively inherited).
4. **Office, city, and location note** — optional, shown on the profile.
5. **Organization metadata** (optional but recommended) — organizational unit, direct manager, contract type, leadership level, leadership category, employer-rights flag.

The dialog supports per-workspace **templates** to speed up repeated invites.
