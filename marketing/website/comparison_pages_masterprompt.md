# Comparison Pages Master Prompt

## Purpose
Guide for creating "Effectime vs. X" and "Effectime vs. manual processes" comparison pages. These pages capture high-intent search traffic from buyers actively evaluating options.

---

## When comparison pages work

Buyers searching "[alternative] vs [Effectime]" or "[category] alternatives" are in active consideration mode. They are comparing options before deciding. A well-built comparison page can capture this intent and win the evaluation.

---

## Neutral-tone principles (non-negotiable)

- Never write comparison pages as attack pieces
- State facts, not opinions about competitors
- Acknowledge where the alternative might genuinely be a better fit
- The goal is trust, not to make competitors look bad

If comparison pages feel like attack pieces, they destroy the trust they're supposed to build.

---

## Priority comparison pages

### 1. Effectime vs. Excel / Spreadsheet-based management

This is not about a competitor — it's about the status quo. Highest conversion potential.

**Headline:** "Effectime vs. spreadsheets: what actually changes"

**Structure:**
- Pain: what breaks in spreadsheet-based people management at > 15 people
- Side-by-side table: 6 scenarios (leave request, coverage check, attendance export, onboarding, multi-site, reporting)
- Outcome: what a real company gained by switching from spreadsheets
- "Not ready to switch? Download our [HR process template] for free."

---

### 2. Effectime vs. Personio (HU/CZ/SK/PL markets)

**Angle:** Personio is a full-stack HRIS targeting > 50-person companies with more complexity and higher price. Effectime is lighter, faster to implement, and operationally focused.

**When Personio wins:** When a company needs deep payroll integration, advanced HRIS workflows, or has > 200 people with a dedicated HR team.

**When Effectime wins:** When a company wants to be operational quickly, doesn't need a 3-month implementation, and is primarily solving coordination / leave / coverage problems.

---

### 3. Effectime vs. Calamari

**Angle:** Calamari is leave and attendance only. Effectime adds coverage planning, multi-site management, resource planning, Jira integration, and HR workflows.

**When Calamari wins:** Simplest possible leave and attendance for a small team with no coordination complexity.

**When Effectime wins:** Any company managing multiple sites, requiring coverage rules, or wanting Jira integration.
