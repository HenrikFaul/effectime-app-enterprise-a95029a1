# Landing Page Master Prompt

## Purpose
Guide for building targeted Effectime landing pages for specific campaigns, personas, or pain points. Each landing page serves a single audience + message combination.

---

## When to build a dedicated landing page

- Paid campaign pointing to a specific audience (not the homepage)
- A specific pain point with enough search volume for SEO
- A feature-focused campaign (e.g., multi-site management)
- An industry-specific page (e.g., for optometry, hospitality)
- Event or webinar registration

---

## Landing page anatomy (conversion-optimized)

### Above the fold (what the visitor sees before scrolling)
- **Headline:** Pain-specific or outcome-specific. NOT the same as the homepage hero.
- **Subheadline:** Clarifies who this is for and what it solves.
- **Primary CTA:** One button. Maximum: two options (primary + secondary).
- **Trust signal:** 1–2 small social proof indicators.

### Problem deepening (one scroll)
- Expand the specific pain. Show you understand their situation precisely.
- Use language from ICP research — the words they actually use.

### Solution promise
- "This is what changes with Effectime." One key capability, outcome-led.

### Feature evidence
- 2–3 specific features that directly address the landing page's pain focus.
- Screenshots or UI illustrations with annotations.

### Proof element
- 1 customer quote or outcome story relevant to this page's audience.
- Quantified outcome if available.

### CTA repeat
- Same CTA as above the fold. Slightly different framing: "Join [X] companies..."

### Objection handler
- 3–4 short objection responses: "No long implementation. No IT required. Export your data anytime."

---

## A/B test suggestions

For any new landing page, test at least one of:
- Above-fold headline (pain-led vs. outcome-led)
- CTA button copy ("Try free" vs. "Start free trial" vs. "Get started")
- Social proof placement (above fold vs. after problem section)

---

## URL and UTM convention

URL structure: `effectime.com/[audience]-or-[pain-topic]`
Example: `effectime.com/multi-site-management`, `effectime.com/leave-management-for-hr`

Always add UTM tags to all ads pointing to this page.
