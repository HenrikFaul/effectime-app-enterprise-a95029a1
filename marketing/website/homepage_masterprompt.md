# Homepage Master Prompt

## Purpose
Full homepage copy structure and writing guide for Effectime. Use alongside `messaging/homepage_messaging.md` for approved headline directions and `visual/website_art_direction.md` for visual direction.

---

## Section 1: Navigation bar

- Logo (left)
- Links: Features / Solutions / Pricing / Resources
- Right: [Log in] [Try free — 14 days]
- Mobile: Hamburger menu; "Try free" always visible as sticky top-right

---

## Section 2: Hero

**Structure:** Headline → Subheadline → Two CTAs → Social proof ticker → Hero visual

**Copy approach:** Problem-led or outcome-led. Never feature-led.

**Headline options:**
- "The workforce coordination system for growing businesses."
- "Stop managing your team through spreadsheets and group chats."
- "Leave, attendance, and coverage — clear, automated, auditable."

**Subheadline:** Names the audience and expands the promise:
> "Effectime gives HR managers, operations teams, and business owners the visibility to coordinate leave, attendance, and coverage — without the complexity of enterprise tools."

**CTA 1 (primary):** "Try free for 14 days →"
**CTA 2 (secondary):** "See how it works" (links to product tour or demo video)

**Social proof ticker:** "[X] companies across [Y] countries · 4.8/5 on G2 · Up and running in [X] days"

---

## Section 3: Problem acknowledgment

**Header:** "Sound familiar?"

Three pain-point cards:
1. "Leave requests arrive by message. Some get lost. Balances are wrong."
2. "You don't know if you're covered until someone doesn't show up."
3. "You're managing 3 offices from 3 different spreadsheets."

---

## Section 4: Solution introduction

**Bridge statement:** "Effectime replaces the scattered, manual, invisible parts of people management with one system that gives the right information to the right person at the right time."

---

## Section 5: Feature highlights (3–4 features)

Each feature block:
- **Outcome headline** (not feature name)
- 2–3 sentence explanation
- Product screenshot (annotated)
- "Learn more" link to solution page

Suggested feature highlights:
1. Leave management: "Every request tracked, every balance accurate."
2. Coverage rules: "Gaps flagged automatically before they become problems."
3. Multi-site management: "All locations, one unified workspace."
4. Time & attendance: "Attendance data clean and payroll-ready."

---

## Section 6: Social proof / case study pull

One-or-two customer story cards. Format:
- Company type + size
- One specific outcome (quantified if possible)
- Pull quote
- "Read the full story" link

---

## Section 7: Trust and reassurance

- "Up and running in days, not months."
- "No IT department required."
- "Your data is always exportable."
- "GDPR-compliant. EU data residency."

---

## Section 8: Closing CTA

**Header:** "Ready to replace the spreadsheet?"
**CTA:** "Start your free 14-day trial"
**Sub-text:** "No credit card required. Cancel anytime. Support available from day one."
