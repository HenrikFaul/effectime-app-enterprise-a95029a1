# Industry Pages Master Prompt

## Purpose
Framework for creating industry-specific landing pages. These pages speak directly to the operational reality of a specific vertical, increasing relevance and conversion for that ICP.

---

## Priority verticals for Effectime

### 1. Optical / optics retail (high priority)
**Pain specific to this vertical:**
- Multi-site; each location has specific equipment (fundus camera, autorefractor, OCT)
- Need qualified optometrists or optical technicians at every site
- Peak periods (back-to-school, year-end) drive leave conflicts
- Coverage rules need to reflect which skills are needed per device per site

**Vocabulary to use:** "üzlet" (store), "optometrista", "szaküzlet", "gép", "telephely lefedettség"

**Key Effectime features to highlight:**
- Office equipment registry with required skill (v3.7.9)
- Minimum staffing per site with skill requirement
- Coverage rules by day and site
- Multi-site management

**Headline direction:** "Manage your optics chain — not just the paperwork."

---

### 2. Healthcare / dental / physiotherapy
**Pain specific to this vertical:**
- Qualified staff minimum requirements per service type
- Leave coverage is a patient safety issue, not just a scheduling preference
- Equipment tied to specific practitioners

**Key features:** Coverage rules with role/skill requirements, min staffing, equipment + required skill

**Headline direction:** "Always the right person in the right place."

---

### 3. Tech companies with Jira
**Pain specific to this vertical:**
- Sprint planning disrupted by leave not integrated with project tools
- Distributed teams; multiple locations; async work
- HR and engineering operate in separate systems

**Key features:** Jira integration for capacity planning, leave calendar, attendance reports

**Headline direction:** "Your sprint plan and your team's availability should talk to each other."

---

### 4. Hospitality (hotels, restaurants, catering)
**Pain specific to this vertical:**
- Shift-based work; daily coverage critical
- High turnover; frequent onboarding
- Seasonal peaks (summer, holidays) drive complex coverage needs

**Key features:** Shift assignments, coverage rules by day, minimum staffing, attendance

**Headline direction:** "When every shift matters, you can't afford coverage surprises."

---

### 5. Multi-site services (cleaning, security, maintenance)
**Pain specific to this vertical:**
- Staff assigned to different client sites per day
- Skill matching between staff and site requirements
- Attendance confirmation at client sites

**Key features:** Multi-site, shift assignments, skill-based coverage

---

## Industry page template

**Structure for each industry page:**
1. Industry-specific headline (pain language, not product language)
2. "For [company type] managing [specific challenge]" subheadline
3. Pain section: 3 specific challenges in their language
4. Solution section: 3 Effectime features translated to their outcomes
5. Screenshot: the specific feature most relevant to this vertical
6. Quote or outcome story from similar company (if available)
7. CTA: "Try free" + "Talk to us about [industry]"
