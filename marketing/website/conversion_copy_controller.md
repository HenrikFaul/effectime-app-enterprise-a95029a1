# Conversion Copy Controller

## Purpose
Master guide for conversion-focused copy across the entire Effectime website. Covers: CTA language, button copy, form optimization, trust signals, and micro-copy throughout the funnel.

---

## CTA language formula

**Formula:** [Action verb] + [Outcome or what they get]

**Good examples:**
- "Start your free trial" (action + what it is)
- "Try free for 14 days" (action + specificity)
- "See it in action" (action + what they get)
- "Get your team organized" (action + outcome)

**Bad examples:**
- "Submit" (no outcome)
- "Learn more" (vague, no commitment)
- "Get started today" (filler word "today" adds nothing)
- "Click here" (describes the mechanic, not the value)

---

## Button copy principles

- Maximum 5 words per button
- Primary button: strongest action + highest clarity
- Secondary button: lower commitment option ("See how it works", "Watch a demo")
- Never two equally strong CTAs on the same screen — one must be primary

---

## Form field reduction strategy

Every additional form field reduces conversion rate by approximately 10–20%.

**Trial signup form — maximum 3 fields:**
1. Work email (required — used for login + segmentation)
2. Company name (optional — nice for personalization)
3. "How did you hear about us?" (optional — attribution)

**Never ask on signup:**
- Phone number (too much commitment for a free trial)
- Company size (can be collected later in onboarding)
- Job title (can be collected later)
- Card details (kills conversion; use free trial without card)

---

## Trust signals — placement and format

**Near primary CTA:**
- "No credit card required."
- "Cancel anytime."
- "14-day free trial."

**Near pricing:**
- "Your data is always exportable."
- "GDPR-compliant. EU data residency."

**In navigation:**
- G2 / Capterra rating badge (if available)
- "Trusted by [X] companies in [Y] countries"

---

## Micro-copy: error states and empty states

**Form validation errors:**
- "Please enter a valid work email." (not "Invalid email format")
- "This field is required." (not "Field cannot be blank")

**Empty states (new user, no data yet):**
- "Add your first office to get started with coverage planning."
- "Invite your team to start managing leave requests together."
Never show a blank screen without a clear next action.

**404 page copy:**
- "This page doesn't exist — but your workforce coordination problems do. Let's fix those."
- CTA: "Go to homepage" + "Try Effectime free"

## Onboarding welcome email (triggered by trial signup)

**Subject:** "Your Effectime workspace is ready — here's where to start"
**From:** Founder name (not "noreply@effectime.com")

**Body:**
> "Hi [name],
> Your workspace is set up. Here's the first thing I'd do:
> [Single most important action based on company size]
> If you need help, reply to this email — I read them.
> [Founder name]"

**Rules:** Personal, short, one action. No feature list. No "We're so excited you joined us."
