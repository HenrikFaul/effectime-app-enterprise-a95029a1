# Content Engine Controller

## Purpose
Master controller for Effectime's content production system. Defines mission, content types, funnel mapping, production cadence, and repurposing logic. Read before producing any content asset.

---

## Content mission

Create content that: (1) educates the target market about operational problems they recognize, (2) builds trust in Effectime as a practical, expert-built solution, (3) captures search demand from people actively looking for solutions, and (4) gives the sales team materials that accelerate decisions.

Content is NOT: a promotional channel for feature announcements, a brand vanity exercise, or a volume game.

---

## Content types and funnel stage

| Content type | Funnel stage | Primary goal | Primary channel |
|-------------|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| Founder LinkedIn posts | Awareness | Build trust + reach ICP | LinkedIn |
| SEO articles (pain-driven) | Awareness + Consideration | Capture search demand | Google |
| Case studies / outcome stories | Consideration + Conversion | Proof | Website, sales, email |
| Email nurture sequences | Consideration | Move from interest to intent | Email |
| Webinars / live content | Consideration | Deepen trust with warm audience | LinkedIn, email |
| Sales enablement content | Conversion | Accelerate decisions | Sales team |
| Comparison content | Conversion | Win consideration stage | Website |

---

## Production cadence (recommended)

| Type | Frequency | Format |
|------|-----------|--------|
| LinkedIn posts (founder) | 3–4/week | 150–400 words |
| LinkedIn posts (brand) | 1–2/week | Visual + short copy |
| SEO articles | 2/month | 1000–2500 words |
| Case study / outcome story | 1/quarter | 600–1200 words |
| Email nurture touchpoint | 1–2/week in sequence | 150–300 words per email |
| Webinar | 1/quarter | 45–60 minute event |

---

## Repurposing logic — 1 to many

Every piece of long-form content should yield multiple short-form assets:

**1 SEO article →**
- 3 LinkedIn posts (one per key insight)
- 1 email to nurture list
- 1 infographic prompt
- 1 pull-quote social card

**1 case study →**
- 1 LinkedIn story post (founder voice)
- 1 email to trial nurture list
- 2 website testimonial pulls
- 1 sales deck proof slide

**1 webinar →**
- 3–5 LinkedIn clips/posts
- 1 summary article
- 3 email touchpoints (pre, live, post)
- 1 gated recording for lead gen

---

## Content quality gate

Before publishing any content:
1. Does it name a specific audience or pain? (not generic)
2. Does it deliver genuine insight or value before asking anything? (not just promotional)
3. Does it align with the narrative house? (not off-brand)
4. Has it been reviewed against governance/terminology_and_positioning_guardrails.md?
5. Does it have a clear purpose (awareness / trust / conversion) — not "just to post something"?

Content that fails any of these is not ready to publish.
