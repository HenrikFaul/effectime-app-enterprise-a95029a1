# Webinar Master Prompt

## Purpose
Framework for planning, scripting, and promoting Effectime webinars. Webinars are a high-trust format used at the consideration stage to deepen credibility with a warm audience.

---

## Webinar positioning

Effectime webinars are educational events, not product demos. The primary goal is to be genuinely useful to attendees whether or not they sign up for Effectime. The secondary goal is to demonstrate that Effectime understands the problem deeply enough to be trusted with the solution.

---

## Approved webinar topics (high-conversion categories)

1. "How to reduce leave management chaos in a 30–150 person company"
2. "Multi-site workforce management: what nobody tells you when you open a second location"
3. "Coverage planning: how to stop being surprised by understaffing"
4. "Building an HR system that runs itself — practical automation for small HR teams"
5. "Sprint planning and people operations: why your capacity planning keeps failing"

---

## Webinar structure (45–60 minutes)

**[0:00–2:00] Hook opener**
Start with a specific, relatable scenario: "Picture this: it's Monday morning and your manager at the second location just called to say three people called in sick..." Do NOT start with "Welcome everyone, my name is..."

**[2:00–7:00] Problem framing**
Walk through the real-world problem the webinar addresses. Use data, stories, and patterns — not abstract generalities. Make the audience feel: "This is exactly what happens to us."

**[7:00–22:00] Education / insight section**
Deliver 3–4 specific, actionable frameworks or insights on the topic. This section must have genuine value independent of Effectime. Do not pitch.

**[22:00–27:00] Product connection**
Transition naturally: "This is exactly the problem Effectime was built to solve. Let me show you how..." Show 2–3 specific screens that address the pain discussed. Keep it brief and concrete.

**[27:00–37:00] Live Q&A or audience input**
Take 5–8 questions. Prioritize questions that give you a chance to deepen the education, not just answer about the product.

**[37:00–40:00] CTA close**
Offer: "If this resonated, try Effectime free for 14 days — link in the description. If you want to talk through your specific situation, book a 20-minute call."

> No slides about our product. Just the frameworks we've learned from [X] companies.

> You'll walk away with [3 specific takeaways].
