# Sales Enablement Content

## Purpose
Guide for creating content that helps the sales process at Effectime. Sales enablement content is used by the founder/sales person during outreach, demos, follow-ups, and proposal stages.

---

## Sales enablement content types

### 1. Battle cards
One-pager per competitor or comparison scenario. Structure: "When a prospect is comparing us to [X]..."
- Key difference (feature or positioning)
- When Effectime wins (specific use case)
- When the alternative might be a better fit (be honest — builds trust)
- Best response to "we already use X"

### 2. Objection response scripts
Written responses to the 5 most common objections. See `sales/objection_handling_masterprompt.md`.

### 3. Post-demo follow-up templates

**Template — Standard follow-up:**
Subject: "Next steps after our call + one thing I forgot to mention"
> "Thanks for the demo time. Based on what you told me about [their specific pain], I wanted to follow up with:
> [Specific relevant feature or case that addresses their situation]
> [Link to start trial or book next call]
> Let me know if any questions come up — happy to jump on a call."

**Template — "No response" follow-up (day 5):**
Subject: "Quick question about [their company name]"
> "Checking back in — did the demo address the [specific problem they mentioned] question?
> If timing isn't right, that's completely fine. Just let me know and I'll check back in [month].
> Otherwise — [link to trial]."

### 4. "What happens after you sign" explainer
A 1-page or short email that walks a prospect through: day 1 setup, week 1 onboarding, what support looks like, what data they need to bring. Reduces the fear of commitment.

### 5. ROI framing reference
Not a formal calculator — a reference for how to quantify value in conversation.
- "If your HR manager spends 4 hours/week on leave coordination, that's [€X/year at their rate]."
- "If one coverage gap costs a day's revenue at a single site, and you have [N] sites..."
- "Most customers tell us they recover 3–6 hours/week in admin time within the first month."

### 6. Comparison one-pager (Effectime vs. manual/spreadsheets)
Not against a named competitor — against "the way you're doing it now."
Structure: side-by-side table of 6 scenarios (leave request, coverage check, attendance report, onboarding new employee, monthly HR report, multi-site coordination).
For each: how long it takes manually vs. with Effectime; what can go wrong manually vs. what's prevented.
