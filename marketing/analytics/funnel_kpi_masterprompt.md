# Funnel KPI Master Prompt

## Purpose
Detailed KPI definitions and benchmarks per funnel stage for Effectime. Use when building dashboards, reviewing performance, setting targets, or planning experiments.

---

## Stage 1: Awareness

**Goal:** Get the right people to know Effectime exists and understand the category problem it solves.

**Primary KPI:** Qualified impressions (reach among ICP: HR/ops/owner roles at 20–300 person companies)
**Secondary KPI:** Content engagement rate
**Benchmark:** LinkedIn organic posts — 2–5% engagement rate; > 500 impressions per post at early stage
**Red flag:** All reach is generic (no ICP signal in who engages)
**Optimization lever:** More specific pain-point content; founder voice vs. brand voice test

---

## Stage 2: Interest

**Goal:** Convert awareness into active curiosity — a visit to the website, a read of content, a follow.

**Primary KPI:** Website sessions from marketing-attributed sources
**Secondary KPI:** Average session duration on feature/solution pages
**Benchmark:** 2–4 minutes average session on solution pages; < 60% bounce on homepage
**Red flag:** High traffic, low engagement (content/ad promise doesn't match landing page)
**Optimization lever:** Better message match between ad/content and landing page

---

## Stage 3: Consideration

**Goal:** Move from "interesting" to "I want to understand this better."

**Primary KPI:** Demo requests or pricing page views
**Secondary KPI:** Content downloads (gated assets, templates, guides)
**Benchmark:** 2–5% of website visitors request demo or download asset
**Red flag:** High pricing page views, zero conversions (pricing objection or missing proof)
**Optimization lever:** Add social proof to pricing page; simplify trial signup CTA

---

## Stage 4: Intent

**Goal:** Convert consideration into a trial signup.

**Primary KPI:** Trial signups (total and qualified)
**Secondary KPI:** Signup form completion rate
**Benchmark:** > 3% of unique website visitors sign up for trial
**Red flag:** High form abandonment (form too long, too much friction)
**Optimization lever:** Reduce form fields to minimum; add trust signal near CTA

---

## Stage 5: Activation

**Goal:** Ensure trial users complete the key action that drives habit and conversion.

**Key activation action:** Invite first team member OR submit first leave request OR run first coverage check
**Primary KPI:** % of trial signups that complete key action within 7 days
**Secondary KPI:** % of features used in first 14 days
**Benchmark:** Target > 50% activation within 7 days
**Red flag:** < 30% activation (onboarding friction or unclear first value moment)
**Optimization lever:** Triggered onboarding email at hour 24 and day 3; product tour; guided setup

---

## Stage 6: Conversion

**Goal:** Convert activated trial to paid account.

**Primary KPI:** Trial-to-paid conversion rate
**Secondary KPI:** Time from signup to first payment
**Benchmark:** B2B SaaS SMB target: 15–25% trial-to-paid at 30 days
**Red flag:** < 10% — pricing, value delivery, or trial length issue
**Optimization lever:** Conversion email sequence; sales touchpoint for high-signal trials; pricing page clarity

---

## Stage 7: Retention

**Goal:** Keep paying customers using and renewing.

**Primary KPI:** Monthly churn rate
**Secondary KPI:** Feature adoption depth (% of paid customers using 3+ modules)
**Benchmark:** < 3% monthly churn for healthy B2B SaaS at this stage
**Red flag:** > 5% monthly churn (product-market fit question or onboarding gap)
**Optimization lever:** Onboarding call at day 14; in-app tips; proactive support for low-usage accounts

---

## Reporting cadence

| Frequency | Review |
|-----------|--------|
| Weekly | Trial signups, activation rate, active trials |
| Monthly | Full funnel health check, channel attribution, churn |
| Quarterly | KPI target review, experiment backlog prioritization, CAC/LTV calculation |
