# Experiment Backlog

## Purpose
Structured backlog of marketing experiments for Effectime. Each experiment follows: hypothesis → test design → success metric → minimum run duration. Use to prioritize which experiments to run and how to interpret results.

---

## Experiment framework

For every experiment:
- **Hypothesis:** "We believe that [change] will cause [outcome] because [reason]."
- **Control:** What is currently running
- **Variant:** What we are testing
- **Success metric:** One primary, measurable KPI
- **Minimum duration:** Long enough to be statistically meaningful (usually 2–4 weeks for content, 100+ conversions for paid)
- **Decision rule:** "If [metric] improves by [X]%, we roll out the variant. If not, we pause and learn."

---

## Experiment backlog

### 1. LinkedIn headline variant test
**Hypothesis:** Pain-specific headlines ("Do your managers know if they're covered tomorrow?") outperform outcome headlines ("Workforce coordination for growing businesses") in engagement rate.
**Metric:** Engagement rate per post (reactions + comments ÷ impressions)
**Duration:** 6 posts each variant, compare average
**Decision:** Roll out winning format as default for founder posts

### 2. Homepage hero A/B
**Hypothesis:** A problem-led headline ("Stop managing your team in WhatsApp") converts better than an outcome-led headline ("One system for leave, attendance, and coverage").
**Metric:** Trial signup rate from homepage
**Duration:** 200 unique homepage visitors per variant
**Decision:** Roll out winning headline; repeat test with subheadline next

### 3. Cold outreach subject line test
**Hypothesis:** Subject lines that reference a specific company-size scenario ("For companies between 30–80 people") outperform generic subject lines ("Simplify your HR processes").
**Metric:** Email open rate
**Duration:** 50 sends per variant, 3 variants
**Decision:** Roll out winner across outreach sequences

### 4. SEO content topic bet
**Hypothesis:** Articles targeting "multi-site leave management" rank and convert better than articles targeting "HR software for SMBs" (too competitive).
**Metric:** Organic ranking within 90 days; sessions per month at 6 months
**Duration:** 90 days post-publish
**Decision:** Double down on niche operational topics vs. broad HR category

### 5. CTA button copy test
**Hypothesis:** "Try free for 14 days" converts better than "Get started" or "Start free trial."
**Metric:** Click-through rate on primary CTA
**Duration:** 200 unique pageviews per variant
**Decision:** Roll out winner across all CTAs

### 6. Trial onboarding email sequence
**Hypothesis:** A 5-email onboarding sequence (day 0, 1, 3, 7, 14) produces higher activation than the current 1-email welcome.
**Metric:** Trial activation rate (key action within 7 days)
**Duration:** 50 trial signups per arm
**Decision:** Roll out winning sequence; test content variants in next round

### 7. LinkedIn ad creative test (image vs. text-only)
**Hypothesis:** Text-only LinkedIn ads with a strong pain-point hook outperform image-based ads for initial engagement at Effectime's awareness stage.
**Metric:** CTR and cost per trial signup
**Duration:** €500 per variant, minimum 2 weeks
**Decision:** Allocate 70% of LinkedIn budget to winner

### 8. Pricing page layout test
**Hypothesis:** A pricing page with a "most popular" plan highlight and a ROI framing ("saves ~5 hrs/week per manager") converts better than a plain feature-comparison table.
**Metric:** Pricing page to trial signup rate
**Duration:** 300 unique pricing page visitors per variant
**Decision:** Roll out winner; test next element (social proof placement)

### 9. Demo format test (live vs. recorded)
**Hypothesis:** A 15-minute recorded product tour converts trial signups at a higher rate than a calendar-booking flow for a live demo (reduces friction for SMB buyers who don't want a sales call).
**Metric:** Demo-to-trial conversion rate
**Duration:** 4 weeks, equal traffic split
**Decision:** Keep whichever drives more trials; reserve live demos for high-intent prospects

### 10. Webinar topic test
**Hypothesis:** "How to reduce leave management chaos in your company" attracts more qualified registrants than "Introduction to Effectime's HR platform."
**Metric:** Registration rate and attendee-to-trial conversion
**Duration:** Run both once; compare
**Decision:** Run educational topic webinars as default; product webinars only for existing warm audiences
