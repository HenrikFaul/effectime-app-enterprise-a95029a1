# Measurement Framework

## Purpose
Defines what Effectime measures, why, and how. Use before setting up any analytics stack, reporting cadence, or performance review. Grounds marketing decisions in numbers that matter at the current stage.

---

## North Star Metric

**Trial signups from qualified sources** — companies with 20–300 employees in target markets (HU, CZ, SK, PL, EN) who signed up through an owned or paid channel.

Secondary North Star: **Trial-to-paid conversion rate** — the percentage of trials that become paying accounts.

---

## Funnel stages and primary KPIs

| Stage | KPI | Secondary KPI | Red flag |
|-------|-----|---------------|----------|
| Awareness | Organic reach, LinkedIn impressions | Content engagement rate | Flat or declining for 8+ weeks |
| Interest | Website sessions, blog/article pageviews | Time on page, scroll depth | Bounce rate > 75% on feature pages |
| Consideration | Demo requests, pricing page views | Content downloads | Demo-to-trial gap > 14 days |
| Intent | Trial signups | Qualified trial signups | High signup, low activation |
| Activation | Trial activation rate (key action in first 7 days) | Features used in trial | < 40% complete first key action |
| Conversion | Trial-to-paid rate | Time to conversion | < 15% trial-to-paid at 30 days |
| Retention | Monthly churn rate | NPS or CSAT | > 5% monthly churn |
| Expansion | Upsell rate, seat growth | Feature adoption over time | No expansion after 90 days |

---

## Channel-level KPIs

### LinkedIn (organic founder content)
- Impressions per post
- Engagement rate (reactions + comments ÷ impressions) — target: > 3%
- Profile views following high-performing post
- Inbound DMs or connection requests with buying intent
- Content-attributed trial signups (UTM or self-reported)

### SEO / organic search
- Keyword ranking position for target terms
- Organic sessions per month
- Organic-to-trial conversion rate
- Top-performing articles by trial attribution

### LinkedIn paid
- Cost per click (target: < €3–5 for B2B SMB)
- Click-through rate (target: > 0.5% for awareness, > 0.8% for conversion)
- Cost per trial signup
- Return on ad spend (ROAS) once conversion data is available

### Email
- Open rate (target: > 35% for owned list)
- Click rate (target: > 5%)
- Reply rate (for direct outreach)
- Email-attributed trial signups

### Retargeting
- Return visit rate from retargeting audience
- Retargeting-attributed trial signups
- Cost per retargeted conversion

---

## Monthly reporting template

Every month, review and document:

1. **Funnel health check** — snapshot of each KPI vs. prior month and target
2. **Top 3 performing assets** — content pieces, ads, or channels driving most qualified traffic
3. **Bottom 3 performers** — what to pause, rethink, or test against
4. **Experiment results** — any A/B tests or channel experiments that concluded
5. **Next month priorities** — which lever to pull based on data
6. **Open questions** — what we still don't know and how to find out

---

## Self-reported attribution

Until a proper attribution tool is in place, add "How did you hear about us?" to the trial signup form. Options: LinkedIn / Google search / referral / blog article / other.

Review self-reported data monthly. It is imperfect but directionally useful for early-stage channel decisions.

---

## What NOT to measure (vanity metrics)

- Social followers (unless growing at unusual rate)
- Total website pageviews (sessions and conversion matter more)
- Total email list size without segmenting by engagement
- LinkedIn likes without looking at trial or pipeline impact
- Press coverage without tracking if it drove any qualified traffic
