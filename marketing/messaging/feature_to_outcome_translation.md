# Feature-to-Outcome Translation Guide

## Purpose
This file is the translation layer between Effectime's technical features and the business outcomes that make buyers act. Use it when writing website copy, creating ads, building sales decks, or scripting demos. Never lead with the feature — always lead with what it replaces or what it achieves. This is the single most important discipline in Effectime marketing copy.

---

## Translation Format
**Feature → What it replaces → Time/error saved → Business outcome → Marketing-ready sentence**

---

## Leave Management

**Feature**: Digital leave request and approval workflow with configurable policies per team or employment type

**What it replaces**: WhatsApp/email/in-person leave requests with no record; manual approval via back-and-forth messages; spreadsheet leave tracker updated after the fact

**Time/error saved**: 2–5 hours per week for HR; zero missed approvals; no month-end reconciliation of "who approved what"

**Business outcome**: Every leave request is documented, approved through one channel, and reflected instantly in the team calendar and coverage view

**Marketing-ready sentences**:
- "Leave requests that go through a system — not a chat thread. Approved, tracked, recorded."
- "Stop approving leave via WhatsApp. Start approving with one click and a full coverage view."
- "Every leave request becomes a record. Not a memory."

---

## Attendance Tracking

**Feature**: Clock-in/clock-out with configurable rules, mobile access, and automatic feed to reporting and payroll export

**What it replaces**: Paper sign-in sheets, Excel time logs, honor-system tracking with no verification, manual data entry before payroll

**Time/error saved**: Payroll reconciliation drops from hours to minutes; zero manual data transfer errors; overtime and lateness tracked automatically

**Business outcome**: Attendance data that is accurate by default, not accurate after manual correction

**Marketing-ready sentences**:
- "Attendance that tracks itself — and feeds directly to your payroll export."
- "Replace your paper sign-in sheet with a record that's audit-ready from day one."
- "Month-end used to mean 3 hours reconciling timesheets. Now it's a report you run in 5 minutes."

---

## Coverage Rules and Scheduling

**Feature**: Minimum staffing rules per role, site, or time period with visual coverage warnings before leave is approved

**What it replaces**: Manual mental calculation ("are we covered if this person is off?"), reactive understaffing discovered the day it happens, manager calls at 7am on a Monday

**Time/error saved**: Coverage failures prevented entirely; on-call scrambling eliminated; planning horizon extended from 1 day to multiple weeks

**Business outcome**: No coverage surprises. The system flags a problem before it becomes an incident.

**Marketing-ready sentences**:
- "See whether approving this leave request creates a coverage problem — before you approve it, not after."
- "Set your minimum staffing rules once. Effectime flags the problems. You make the call."
- "Coverage planning that happens in the system, not in someone's head."

---

## Org Chart

**Feature**: Visual, always-current organizational chart that reflects the live team structure, reporting lines, and role assignments

**What it replaces**: Outdated PowerPoint org charts; onboarding confusion; manual updates after every hire or reorganization

**Time/error saved**: New hires can self-orient; management doesn't spend time explaining structure; no stale org chart in investor decks

**Business outcome**: A single source of truth for team structure, always current, visible to everyone with appropriate access

**Marketing-ready sentences**:
- "An org chart that updates when people join, leave, or move — not six months later."
- "Your team structure, live. Not the version from last quarter."

---

## Agile / Jira Integration

**Feature**: Leave and availability data visible inside Jira sprint planning; capacity warnings when team members are marked absent during sprint

**What it replaces**: Sprint planning that ignores HR reality; developers assigned to sprints while on leave; separate HR and project conversations

**Time/error saved**: Sprint planning sessions that don't require an HR data cross-check; fewer sprint failures from absence-related capacity shortfalls

**Business outcome**: HR data and project planning live in the same context — no more planning against ghost capacity

**Marketing-ready sentences**:
- "Plan sprints with full visibility of who's actually available — not just who's on the team."
- "Jira knows your sprint. Effectime makes sure Jira also knows who's on leave."
- "The gap between your HR system and your project board, closed."

---

## Resource Planning

**Feature**: Capacity view across teams and time periods, showing availability, planned leave, and utilization by role or project

**What it replaces**: Ad hoc capacity checks via email/Slack; project managers discovering absences during planning calls; resource spreadsheets updated by hand

**Time/error saved**: Project planning with accurate capacity from the start; fewer mid-project surprises; better utilization visibility

**Business outcome**: Resource decisions grounded in real availability data, not estimates

**Marketing-ready sentences**:
- "Know your actual team capacity before you commit to a deadline."
- "Resource planning that starts from real data, not a guess followed by a phone call."

---

## HR Workflows

**Feature**: Configurable HR process workflows (onboarding, offboarding, document collection, policy acknowledgments) with automated task routing

**What it replaces**: Email chains for document collection; tasks that fall through the cracks when an HR person is absent; inconsistent onboarding experience

**Time/error saved**: Onboarding tasks tracked automatically; no "did we forget to collect the signed contract?" moments

**Business outcome**: Consistent, documented HR processes that run the same way every time

**Marketing-ready sentences**:
- "Onboarding that runs the same way every time — without someone manually chasing every step."
- "HR workflows that don't depend on one person remembering to send the email."

---

## Multi-Office / Multi-Site Management

**Feature**: Site-specific configurations, per-location leave policies, coverage rules per site, cross-site visibility dashboard

**What it replaces**: Site-specific Excel files; different managers using different processes; owner/HR blind to status at other locations

**Time/error saved**: No manual consolidation of per-site data; consistent policy enforcement; cross-site coverage gaps visible in one dashboard

**Business outcome**: Operate multiple locations with the same clarity and process discipline as a single office

**Marketing-ready sentences**:
- "Two offices, five offices, or ten — one place to see who's working and who's covered."
- "Same process, every location. No more site-by-site spreadsheet chaos."

---

## Import / Export

**Feature**: Import employee data from spreadsheets; export attendance and leave data to payroll formats; Excel-compatible outputs

**What it replaces**: Manual data re-entry between systems; copy-paste errors on payroll day; data locked inside a system that doesn't talk to anything

**Time/error saved**: System setup in hours not days; payroll export in minutes not hours; zero manual data transfer for existing data

**Business outcome**: Effectime fits into the existing workflow — no rip-and-replace required

**Marketing-ready sentences**:
- "Bring your team data in from Excel. Send payroll data out in the format your accountant needs."
- "No migration horror story. Import your existing data and be live in hours."

---

## Reporting Dashboard

**Feature**: Pre-built HR and operational reports — absence rates, attendance patterns, leave balance summaries, coverage history, team utilization

**What it replaces**: Manual report construction in Excel every month; inconsistent data depending on who runs the report; waiting for HR to pull data before making a decision

**Time/error saved**: Monthly HR reports in minutes; leadership gets data on demand; audit responses in hours not days

**Business outcome**: Decisions made on accurate, real-time data — not a spreadsheet snapshot from last Friday

**Marketing-ready sentences**:
- "Your HR dashboard, live. No manual report construction. No waiting."
- "When the CEO asks for last quarter's absence data, you pull the report — you don't build it."
- "Audit-ready records, always. Not just before the audit."
