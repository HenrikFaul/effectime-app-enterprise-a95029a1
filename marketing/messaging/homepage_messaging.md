# Homepage Messaging Framework

## Purpose
Defines the approved message architecture for the Effectime homepage. Every element — headline, subheadline, section headers, CTAs — must fit within this framework. Use before writing any homepage copy or reviewing homepage drafts.

---

## Hero section messaging

### Primary headline goal
Communicate the core value in one sentence that a non-technical manager can instantly understand. No jargon, no feature list, no hype.

### Approved headline directions
- "Finally, a clear view of your whole team — leave, attendance, coverage, all in one place."
- "Stop managing your team through spreadsheets and group chats."
- "Workforce coordination that actually works at your company's size."
- "Less operational chaos. More clarity for your managers."

### Subheadline goal
Name the audience and expand the promise with one additional specificity.

### Approved subheadline directions
- "Effectime gives HR managers, operations teams, and business owners the visibility and tools to coordinate people, time, and coverage — without enterprise complexity."
- "Built for companies between 20 and 300 people who've outgrown spreadsheets but don't need a 6-month HRIS implementation."

### CTA
Primary: "Start free" or "Try free for 14 days"
Secondary: "See how it works" (links to demo or product tour)
Avoid: "Get started today", "Learn more", "Sign up now" (too generic)

---

## Social proof / trust bar (below hero)
Show: company type indicators (not logos if confidential), countries/languages, a simple stat ("Used by teams across 5 countries").

---

## Problem section messaging

Establish 3 pain points. Each should be one line that a target buyer reads and thinks: "yes, that's exactly our problem."

**Approved pain statements:**
1. "Leave requests arrive by message, get lost, or get disputed."
2. "Scheduling meetings happen without knowing who's actually available."
3. "Managers at different locations have no shared view of coverage or capacity."

---

## Solution introduction messaging

Transition from pain to solution with a bridge statement.

**Approved bridge directions:**
- "Effectime replaces the scattered, manual, invisible parts of people management with one system that gives everyone the right information at the right time."
- "Not another HR platform. A coordination system designed for the way growing businesses actually work."

---

## Feature highlight section messaging (3–4 features)

Each feature highlight must follow: **Outcome headline → brief explanation → visual/screenshot**

Never lead with the feature name. Always lead with the outcome.

| Feature area | Outcome headline |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Leave management | "Every request tracked, every balance accurate, no surprises." |
| Coverage rules | "Always know if you're covered — before the day starts." |
| Multi-site management | "All your locations. One unified view." |
| Time & attendance | "Attendance tracked and payroll-ready. No spreadsheet required." |
| Reporting | "The reports leadership actually needs. Ready in seconds." |

---

## Trust section messaging

**Primary trust claims (use only what can be substantiated):**
- "Your data is always yours — full export at any time."
- "Up and running in days, not months."
- "No IT department required."
- "GDPR-compliant. EU data residency."

---

## Closing CTA messaging

Reinforce the primary CTA with a low-friction framing:
- "Try it free for 14 days. No credit card required. Cancel anytime."
- Under the CTA: "Questions? Talk to a human. [link to contact]"
