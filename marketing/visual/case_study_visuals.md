# Case Study Visuals

## Purpose
Visual templates and AI prompts for Effectime case study and customer story assets. Covers: full PDF case study, 1-page highlight card, LinkedIn post visual, and website block.

---

## Full case study PDF layout (A4 / letter)

**Page 1 — Cover:**
- Large headline: "[Company type] reduced [problem] with Effectime"
- Key metric pulled out: "[X hours/week saved]" in large type
- Company context (size, industry, markets — no name if confidential)
- Effectime brand mark

**Page 2 — The problem:**
- Section header: "The challenge"
- 2–3 paragraphs, customer voice
- Quote pull-out from customer

**Page 3 — The solution:**
- Section header: "How they solved it"
- Feature walkthrough: 2–3 specific capabilities used
- Product screenshot with annotation

**Page 4 — The result:**
- Section header: "What changed"
- 3 specific outcomes (quantified where possible)
- Second customer quote
- CTA: "Try Effectime free for 14 days"

**AI prompt for cover visual:**
> "Professional case study report cover, clean minimal design, dark navy header section, white content area, subtle geometric accent, space for headline text and metric highlight, professional B2B document aesthetic, no photos"

---

## LinkedIn case study post visual (1200×627px)

**AI prompt:**
> "Clean horizontal card design, left side navy with white headline text, right side white with key metric in large navy typography, thin brand accent dividing line, professional minimal B2B style, no photos"

**Text spec:**
- Left side: 6-word max headline ("How [company type] fixed their [problem]")
- Right side: one metric ("6 hrs/week saved") in 48px bold

---

## Website feature block (full-width, 2-column)

Left column: customer quote + company context
Right column: annotated product screenshot showing the specific feature they used

**Visual treatment:**
- Light gray section background
- Quote in large serif-style quotation marks (brand color)
- Screenshot with subtle drop shadow
- CTA button below: "Read the full story"
