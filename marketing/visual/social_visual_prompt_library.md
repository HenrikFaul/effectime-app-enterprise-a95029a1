# Social Visual Prompt Library

## Purpose
AI prompt library for LinkedIn social media visuals. Covers: thought-leadership post images, carousel slides, stat cards, and quote cards. Includes dimensions and style specs.

---

## LinkedIn thought-leadership post image (1200×628px)

Used as a header image on long-form articles or as standalone visual posts.

**Style:** Minimalist, text-dominant, brand-colored. No photography. Clean and professional.

**AI prompt template:**
> "Minimal professional header image, deep navy background, clean geometric subtle texture, space for large white text overlay, no people or photos, professional B2B SaaS aesthetic, 1200x628 pixels landscape format"

**Text overlay spec:**
- Title: 32–40px, bold, white
- Subtitle: 18–22px, regular, light blue-gray
- Bottom accent line: brand color strip at bottom

---

## LinkedIn carousel slides (1080×1080px)

Used for multi-slide educational posts. Each slide = one idea, one visual treatment.

**Cover slide AI prompt:**
> "Clean square graphic, deep navy background, bold white headline text area, small accent color strip or shape at bottom-left, professional minimal design, no photos, B2B SaaS style, generous whitespace"

**Content slide AI prompt:**
> "Clean infographic-style square, white background, single data point or short list, navy typography, brand accent color for emphasis, professional sans-serif layout, no decorative elements, minimal and clear"

**Text spec per slide:**
- Cover: max 8 words headline, 5 word subheading
- Content: max 40 words per slide
- Always include slide number: "2/7" in corner

---

## Stat card (1080×1080px or 800×420px)

Used for data-driven social posts: performance numbers, industry benchmarks, or customer outcomes.

**AI prompt:**
> "Bold data visualization card, large percentage or number as hero element, clean professional design, navy and white, one accent color highlight, sans-serif typography, minimal supporting text, generous whitespace, professional B2B aesthetic"

**Rules:**
- One big number per card
- Qualifying text in small font below
- Source citation if external data
- Effectime brand mark at bottom

---

## Quote card (1200×628px)

Used for customer testimonial pulls or thought-leadership quotes from the founder.

**AI prompt:**
> "Clean quote card design, elegant professional style, large quotation mark in brand color, white or very light background, generous text space, small attribution area at bottom, minimal decorative elements, 1200x628 landscape"

**Text spec:**
- Quote: 20–25px, regular, max 150 characters for legibility
- Attribution: name, role/company type (not full company name unless approved)
- Quotation mark: brand accent color, 80–100px
