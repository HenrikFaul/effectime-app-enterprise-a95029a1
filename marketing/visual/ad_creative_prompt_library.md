## Copy overlay specs (all formats)

- Headline: maximum 40 characters for strong impact
- Body: maximum 100 characters (LinkedIn truncates beyond this)
- CTA: 2–4 words maximum ("Try free", "See how it works", "Start free trial")
- Text over image: always ensure WCAG AA contrast ratio
- Brand color on text: navy on white, white on navy — never low-contrast combinations
