# Infographic Prompt Library

## Purpose
AI prompt library for infographic-style content. Covers Effectime-relevant data topics with visual layout descriptions and recommended distribution channels.

---

## Infographic 1: "The hidden cost of manual leave management"

**Data structure:**
- Time spent per week on manual leave coordination (estimate by company size)
- Number of errors per month in manual tracking
- Cost per error (estimated payroll or compliance cost)
- Total annual cost estimate

**Visual layout:** Horizontal timeline from "leave request received" to "leave recorded in payroll" — showing each manual step and potential failure point.

**AI prompt:**
> "Clean professional infographic, navy and white color scheme, horizontal flow diagram showing 8-step process with failure points highlighted in amber, numbered steps with icons, professional B2B data visualization style, no photos, clean sans-serif typography"

**Distribution:** LinkedIn carousel, website blog header, downloadable gated asset

## Infographic 2: "What coordination chaos actually costs per week"

**Data structure:**
- HR manager time on coordination vs. strategic work (pie chart)
- Manager time checking availability vs. doing management work
- Team time waiting for approvals

**Visual layout:** Three side-by-side stat blocks with illustrations of time allocation.

**AI prompt:**
> "Three-panel comparison infographic, clean minimal design, white background, navy and amber accent colors, each panel showing percentage stat with simple icon, professional B2B data visualization, generous whitespace, sans-serif typography"

**Distribution:** LinkedIn post, email newsletter, sales deck

## Infographic 3: "Coverage planning: the 6 things most managers miss"

**Data structure:**
- List of 6 common coverage gaps (skill requirements, holiday conflicts, part-time patterns, leave adjacency, minimum headcount by day, multi-site coordination)
- Brief explanation per item

**Visual layout:** Numbered list with icon per item, clean and readable on mobile.

**AI prompt:**
> "Numbered list infographic, 6 items, navy header with white number, clean white cards for each item, small functional icon per item, professional B2B style, mobile-readable proportions, 800x1800px tall format"

**Distribution:** LinkedIn (native image), Pinterest, downloadable checklist

## Infographic 4: "From request to approval: 5 steps vs. 15 steps"

**Data structure:**
- Old process (with Effectime): 5 clear steps
- Manual process: 15+ steps including back-and-forth, message threads, manual recording

**Visual layout:** Two columns, side by side. Left = old way (messy, many steps in amber/red). Right = new way (clean, 5 steps in navy/green).

**AI prompt:**
> "Side-by-side comparison infographic, left column titled 'Without a system' showing tangled flow with many steps in amber/red, right column titled 'With Effectime' showing clean 5-step flow in navy and green, professional B2B minimal design, equal column width, clear labels"

**Distribution:** LinkedIn carousel (one slide per comparison), sales deck, website feature page
