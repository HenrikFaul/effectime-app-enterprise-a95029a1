# Event-Based Marketing

## Purpose
Campaign framework for event-triggered marketing at Effectime. Events create natural hooks for content and outreach with genuine relevance — not forced campaign angles.

---

## Event categories and playbooks

### Product launch / feature release

**Trigger:** New major feature or module shipped
**Audience:** Existing users + warm prospects + cold ICP via LinkedIn
**Message:** What new problem this feature solves (outcome-first, not feature-first)
**Channel sequence:**
1. Email to existing users and trial leads (day of launch)
2. LinkedIn founder post (day of launch)
3. LinkedIn brand post with visual (day 2)
4. Short LinkedIn ad if budget available (days 3–14)
5. Blog post / SEO article on the solved problem (week 2)

**Copy angle:** "A problem we kept hearing about from customers: [X]. We built a way to solve it."

---

### Year-end / Q4 (HR planning season)

**Trigger:** October–November — companies plan HR processes for next year
**Audience:** HR managers, ops managers, business owners at 30–150 person companies
**Message:** "Next year, start with systems — not spreadsheets."
**Channel sequence:**
1. LinkedIn content series: "5 HR processes to fix before January" (weeks 1–4)
2. Email to cold list with gated asset: "HR planning checklist for [year]"
3. Retargeting ads to website visitors from Q3
4. LinkedIn paid to ICP titles in Oct–Nov

---

### Summer / peak leave season

**Trigger:** May–July — leave requests peak, coverage anxiety peaks
**Audience:** HR managers and operations managers
**Message:** "When the whole team wants August off, who's actually keeping things running?"
**Channel sequence:**
1. LinkedIn awareness posts (April–May)
2. Targeted outreach/email to known ICP leads
3. Retargeting with coverage-focused creative

---

### Industry events (HR Tech, etc.)

**Trigger:** Major HR or SaaS industry event
**Audience:** Event attendees, people following the event hashtag
**Message:** Relevant, timely take on the event topic — not a promotional post
**Note:** Effectime doesn't need to sponsor events to benefit. Commentary and insights around events create awareness with a warm audience.

---

### EU/local labor compliance deadlines

**Trigger:** Upcoming regulatory change affecting leave, attendance, or working hours in HU/CZ/SK/PL
**Audience:** HR managers and owners in affected markets
**Message:** "This change affects how you track [X]. Here's what you need to know."
**Note:** Always have legal review before publishing compliance-adjacent content. Frame as "things to be aware of" not "we guarantee compliance."
