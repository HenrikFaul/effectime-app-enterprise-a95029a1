# Guerrilla Marketing Master Prompt

## Purpose
Low-budget, high-creativity tactics for growing Effectime's awareness and trial pipeline at founder stage. These tactics prioritize real value delivery over reach, and genuine relationship over broadcast.

---

## LinkedIn comment strategy

Identify the top 20 HR managers, operations leads, and SMB owners in your target markets who post regularly on LinkedIn. Follow them. Leave thoughtful, specific comments on posts that relate to operational challenges, workforce management, or team coordination.

**Rules:**
- Comment adds genuine perspective — not "great post!" or a pitch
- Never drop a link to Effectime in a comment (spammy, penalized)
- Build familiarity over 2–4 weeks before making any direct contact

**Goal:** 5–10 meaningful LinkedIn relationships per month that eventually become warm outreach

---

## Community presence (high-intent audiences)

Target communities where HR/ops professionals gather and ask questions:

- **Facebook groups:** "HR szakemberek" (HU), Czech/Slovak HR professional groups
- **LinkedIn groups:** HR management, operations management, SMB leadership
- **Reddit:** r/humanresources, r/smallbusiness, r/sysadmin (for IT-adjacent tools)
- **Slack communities:** HR/ops professional communities in CEE markets

**Approach:** Answer questions genuinely. Be the most helpful person in the thread. Never pitch. Mention Effectime only when directly relevant and asked about tools.

---

## Cold outreach with genuine value

Not mass email. Targeted, personal outreach to 10–20 specific contacts per week.

**Trigger:** A prospect posted about a problem Effectime solves (leave chaos, scheduling, multi-site coordination).

**Template:**
> "Hi [name], saw your post about [specific thing]. We built something that specifically addresses [their problem]. Happy to share what we learned — no pitch, just a conversation if you're curious. [link to book 20 min call]."

**Rule:** Every outreach must reference something specific about the recipient. Generic mass outreach is explicitly not this strategy.

---

## Integration marketplace listings

List Effectime on:
- Jira Marketplace / Atlassian Marketplace (integration exists — Jira is in the product)
- Google Workspace Marketplace (if calendar sync is available)
- Any HR software directory that allows free listings (Capterra, G2, GetApp, HRTech)

**Goal:** Passive discovery from buyers actively searching for tools in these directories.

---

## Referral mechanics

Build a lightweight referral system:
- Ask satisfied customers directly: "Do you know any other HR managers or owners who deal with the same problem?"
- Offer a month free for both referrer and referred (simple, low-cost)
- Follow up personally after referrals — not automated

---

## Partnership content swaps

Identify 5–10 adjacent but non-competing SaaS tools used by the same ICP (payroll tools, project management, communication tools). Propose a co-authored piece or newsletter swap.

**Example:** "We write about how leave management connects to payroll accuracy for your audience; you write about your [adjacent tool] for our audience."

**Effort:** Low. Returns: new audience exposure, backlinks, community trust.
