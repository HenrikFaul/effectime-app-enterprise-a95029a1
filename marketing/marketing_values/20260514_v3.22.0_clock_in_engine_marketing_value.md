# v3.22.0 — GPS / NFC / QR Clock-In engine — Marketing Value

## One-sentence description

Smartphone-based attendance verification that replaces physical
time-clock hardware — geofenced GPS, rotating-QR-code scanning, NFC
tag tap-in, all server-validated to prevent spoofing.

## Personas helped

- **Frontline workers** — clock in without finding the wall device,
  without an employee badge, without a queue. Their phone IS the
  time clock.
- **Operations managers** — no more time-clock hardware to maintain
  (Dormakaba / HID Global typically charge €500–€3,000 per device +
  annual service contracts).
- **Compliance officers** — ECJ C-55/18 ("Deutsche Bank") requires
  employers to track all working time. Effectime's clock-in is
  audit-ready with verified flag + geofence distance + immutable log.

## Claim types enabled

- **Hardware displacement** — €500–€3,000 per site saved.
- **Regulatory compliance** — EU working-time recording mandate met
  without legacy WFM (ZEUS, TimeSoft) infrastructure.
- **Anti-spoof story** — server-side geofence validation is genuinely
  more secure than the typical "client-side timestamp" model used by
  legacy tools.

## Content angles

1. **DACH-focused content** — German-language post about §3 ArbZG +
   ECJ Deutsche Bank ruling + how Effectime meets both with
   smartphone-only verification.
2. **Procurement comparison** — Effectime clock-in (€0 hardware) vs.
   Dormakaba devices (€500–€3,000 per site) at scale: 100-site
   operator saves €50k–€300k upfront + service contracts.
3. **Demo video** — 30-second clip showing GPS clock-in succeeding
   inside geofence, then failing outside it. Spoof-resistance made
   visible.
