# v3.18.0 — Gamification & engagement layer — Marketing Value

## Feature name and version

Achievement badges + engagement streaks for paying-tier workspaces (v3.18.0).
First concrete delivery against the *Effectime Top-20 Value-Rocket Strategy*
(Rank 14 — Gamification & Employee Engagement).

## One-sentence user-language description

Members on Pro and Enterprise plans now earn visible achievement badges
and see their personal streaks (punctuality, leave planning, coverage
hero) — engagement that drives retention without a single leaderboard or
surveillance prompt.

## Problem it replaces

Workforce scheduling tools are typically opened 2–3 times per week
(check schedule, submit leave, done). That low engagement floor makes it
easy for a customer to swap to a competitor — there's no muscle-memory,
no daily reason to return. Industry benchmark (Octalysis / Salesforce
Trailhead): well-implemented opt-in gamification lifts DAU/MAU by 2–3×.

## Primary beneficiary personas

- **Frontline employees (factory, retail, healthcare, F&B)** — get
  visible recognition for punctuality and reliability without their
  manager having to remember to thank them.
- **Workspace owners** — see team engagement health without surveillance
  optics; engagement signals are mastery-oriented, not shame-oriented.
- **CHRO / People Ops** — can finally tie scheduling-platform
  engagement to ESG / wellbeing dashboards without a separate tool.

## Marketing-ready claim

"Engagement built on mastery, not surveillance. Effectime v3.18 ships
opt-in achievement badges and streaks aligned with self-determination
theory — the only WFM gamification feature designed for European work
culture, not American leaderboard pressure."

## Claim types enabled

- **Cultural fit (EU)** — opt-in per workspace AND per member; no public
  shaming, no leaderboards. Defensible position vs. US-style competitors
  (When I Work, Homebase) that put leaderboards front-and-center.
- **Retention** — daily engagement reason → muscle memory → competitive
  switching cost.
- **Tier upsell driver** — Freemium intentionally excluded; this is a
  Pro+/Enterprise-only feature.
