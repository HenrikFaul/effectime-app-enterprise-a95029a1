# Marketing Value — v3.7.9: Extended Office Parameters

**Date:** 2026-05-12
**Version:** v3.7.9
**Engineering artifact:** `versioning/12052601_v3.7.9_office_extended_params.md`

---

## Feature summary

Extended the office/location management module with five new parameter categories: contact information (email, phone), management contacts (manager name, deputy name), structured opening hours (per day with Closed toggle), equipment/facilities inventory with required-skill linkage, and permanent minimum staffing requirements per site.

---

## User-language description

Each office location now has a complete operational profile: who to call, when it's open, what equipment it has, who needs to be there at minimum, and who runs it — all in one place, always up to date.

---

## Problem it replaces

Multi-site managers previously tracked this information in a mix of: a shared spreadsheet with branch contact details, a physical binder of equipment lists, a WhatsApp group conversation about who covers what, and verbal knowledge about "we always need at least one optometrist on duty." None of this was connected to the HR system, none of it was automatically available when scheduling, and none of it was auditable.

---

## Primary beneficiary personas

1. **Operations manager / multi-site manager** — finally has a single source of truth for each location's operational profile
2. **HR manager** — can see minimum staffing requirements alongside leave and attendance to spot conflicts
3. **Business owner** — can delegate location management with confidence that parameters are documented and consistent
4. **Team lead / store manager** — can see their location's setup clearly without asking the central office
