## 1. One-sentence user-language description

Effectime's AI scheduling copilot, AI document polisher, and direct user provisioning now work end-to-end with no missing backend — managers get AI-powered planning insights in real time while HR admins can provision team members and polish documents without hitting errors.

---

## 2. Problem it replaces

Previously, three product features were silently broken at runtime:
- The AI scheduling copilot returned a 404 (function not deployed) instead of workforce planning insights
- The "Create User" dialog in workspace admin failed with a network error (function not deployed)
- The AI document polish button did nothing (function not deployed)
- The Analytics Dashboard coverage rules widget returned empty results (view missing from DB)
- The superadmin email queue widget logged errors on every load (table missing from DB)

Users saw no actionable error messages — features simply appeared broken or empty.

## 3. Primary beneficiary personas

- **HR Manager / Workspace Owner**: Can now use the AI scheduling copilot to get leave capacity analysis, coverage recommendations, and compliance risk flags from a chat-style interface.
- **Operations Manager**: Direct user provisioning ("Create User" mode in Invite Member dialog) now actually works — reduces new-hire onboarding friction.
- **HR Document Author**: AI document polishing is live — one click turns a raw HR template into a polished, professional document.
- **Superadmin / Platform Admin**: Coverage rules analytics and email queue monitoring now render correctly — reduces operational blind spots.
