**Date:** 2026-05-15

---

## User-language problem solved

> "I opened the backlog and it was empty — I didn't know I had to click search first."  
> "I can't tell which of these 80 discovered fields my team actually uses."

The backlog was silent by default, forcing users to run a manual search before seeing any data. Field selection was a blind choice from a flat list with no usage signal.

---

## Personas who benefit

- **Project lead / Scrum master**: Immediately sees the 10 most recent issues when opening the backlog — no extra steps.
- **Integration admin**: Discovers at a glance which fields are populated in real issues; selects only those for the board in one click ("Select all used").
- **Power user**: Adjusts pagination to 50 or ALL when needing a broad view; returns to 10 for daily triage.
