# Marketing Value — v3.39.0 Smart Staffing Workflow

## Feature name and version
Smart Staffing Workflow (availability + open-shift broadcast + first-claim) — v3.39.0

## One-sentence user-language description
Employees tap which days they're available, managers see that availability when building schedules, and unfilled shifts can be broadcast to the whole team for instant first-come-first-served self-assignment.

## Problem it replaces
Multi-location managers were running shift scheduling through Excel files and WhatsApp/email threads. Employees had no digital way to signal availability. When a shift went unfilled, managers had to individually call or message people to find a replacement. The result: late rosters, repeated follow-ups, and last-minute no-shows.

## Primary beneficiary personas
- **Location Manager / Branch Head** — sees which employees marked themselves available before assigning, gets notified the moment a broadcast shift is claimed, spends less time on roster phone calls
- **Frontline Employee (deskless/hourly)** — marks availability on mobile in seconds, sees all open shifts, claims with one tap and gets instant confirmation — no inbox hunting
- **HR / Operations Lead** — workforce data is in the system before the schedule is built, not reconstructed after the fact

## Marketing-ready claims
- "Employees signal availability in seconds; managers see it instantly when building the schedule."
- "Broadcast unfilled shifts to your whole team — the first person to claim it is automatically assigned."
- "Replace last-minute WhatsApp chains with a structured, digital availability and open-shift workflow."
- "Smart scheduling now surfaces self-marked-available employees first — the right person, automatically ranked higher."

## Claim types enabled
- **Operational efficiency** — scheduler time on fill-calls reduced; last-minute scramble replaced by structured flow
- **Employee experience** — workers have agency and visibility; they choose to claim, not wait to be called
- **Data quality** — availability is recorded before the schedule, not guessed
- **Mobile-first / deskless worker** — tap-to-cycle calendar and one-tap claim work on any screen size
