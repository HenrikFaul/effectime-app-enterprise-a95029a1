# Marketing Value — v3.40.0 Structured Open Shifts

**Version:** 3.40.0  
**Date:** 2026-05-17  
**Feature slug:** structured-open-shifts

---

## User-language problem solved

Managers were typing free-text position names to broadcast open shifts — meaning the system had no idea who actually qualified. The entire workforce got notified about shifts they couldn't fill, or the right people were missed entirely. There was no way to target the best candidates first, no configurable urgency deadline, and no safety net: if the assigned employee cancelled, the shift was simply lost with no automatic recovery.

---

## Personas who benefit

| Persona | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| Shift managers (all locales) | Select position from structured catalog instead of typing free text; see top-3 eligible candidates ranked by availability, skills, and leave status before broadcasting |
| Resourceful admins / schedulers | Configurable escalation timeout (default 3h) automatically widens the search batch-by-batch without manual follow-up |
| Employees (all locales) | Can join a waitlist for filled shifts — automatically promoted when the original assignee cancels, with in-app notification |
| Operations leads | Guaranteed replacement search on cancellation; if no replacement is found, managers are automatically alerted |
