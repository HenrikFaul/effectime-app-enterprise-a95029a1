# v3.49.2 — A11y B1: mobile viewport fix

## Problem solved (user language)
On mobile browsers, parts of the app (login, dashboard, embed views, landing)
were getting hidden behind the phone's address bar. Users had to scroll
awkwardly or could not reach the primary action.

## Personas who benefit
- Field managers / shift leads opening the app on a phone between tasks
- Employees submitting a leave request from a mobile browser
- Candidates opening the public Candidate Book link
- Anyone embedding Effectime in an iframe on a mobile device

## Marketing claims this enables
- "True mobile-first layout — no content trapped behind the browser bar."
- "WCAG 2.2 Reflow compliant across all primary surfaces."

## Content angles
- Short LinkedIn post: "Small fix, big mobile UX win — 16 surfaces upgraded
  to dynamic viewport units."
- A11y commitment thread: B-phase progress (109 icon labels → next).
