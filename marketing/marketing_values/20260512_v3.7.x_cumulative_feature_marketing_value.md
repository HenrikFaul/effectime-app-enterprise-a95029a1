## Feature: Leave Management System (core module)

**Version:** v3.0+
**User-language description:** Employees request leave through the app; managers approve or reject with one click; all requests are tracked, timestamped, and searchable.

**Problem it replaces:** Leave requests arriving via WhatsApp, email, verbal conversation, or sticky notes — with no central record, no audit trail, and no visibility for anyone but the person who received the request.

**Primary personas:** HR manager, team lead, company owner

**Marketing-ready claim:** "Every leave request, approval, and balance — in one place, accessible to everyone who needs it, with no manual tracking."

**Claim types enabled:** Error reduction, coordination clarity, audit trail, time saving (no chasing approvals by message)

**Content angles:**
- "How many leave requests did your team handle in a message thread this month?" (LinkedIn)
- "The real cost of informal leave approval: 3 risks most managers don't track" (SEO article)
- "Before Effectime: leave management in a WhatsApp group. After: one tap, full history." (case study hook)
- "Why HR managers still lose leave requests in 2026" (LinkedIn thought-leadership)

**Funnel stage:** Awareness + Consideration

## Feature: Coverage Rules & Scheduling (v3.5+)

**Version:** v3.5+
**User-language description:** Managers set rules for each office: which positions or skills must be present on which days, and in what minimum numbers. The system shows coverage gaps automatically.

**Problem it replaces:** Managers manually checking who is on leave, who has what skills, and whether enough qualified staff will be present — usually done the morning of, with no forward visibility.

**Primary personas:** Operations manager, store/branch manager, HR with multi-site responsibility

**Claim types enabled:** Operational visibility, error prevention, scheduling confidence, compliance (minimum staffing requirements), capacity planning
