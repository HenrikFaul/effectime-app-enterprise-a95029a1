# Marketing Value — v3.50.0 Embeddable smart-scheduling wizard + live calendar

**Feature name / version:** Embedded Smart-Schedule Wizard (modal) + Embedded Live Calendar Timeline — v3.50.0

## One-sentence user-language description
Anyone can drop a live Effectime workforce calendar — and a one-click "smart scheduling wizard" — straight onto their own website or intranet, and it looks and works exactly like the real Effectime app.

## Problem it replaces
Until now the embeddable widget could show capacity numbers but the **smart auto-scheduler appeared as a stripped-down side panel** and **the rich month calendar (with filters) wasn't available at all** outside Effectime. Teams that wanted partners, clients, or other departments to see and act on the schedule had to send them into the full app behind a login. Embeds felt like a cut-down preview, not the real thing.

## Primary beneficiary persona(s)
- **Ops / workforce manager** — wants to expose a live, filterable staffing calendar on an internal portal without giving everyone an app login.
- **Agency / franchise owner** — embeds the scheduler on a partner site so partners can see coverage and (with a write token) fill gaps with the AI wizard.
- **HR manager** — shares a read-only absence/leave calendar on the intranet, filterable by team, position, location, skill.

## Marketing-ready claim
"Embed your full scheduling suite anywhere — including the AI shift-fill wizard and a filterable live calendar — with two lines of code. Your guest page mirrors Effectime's exact look, feel, and intelligence."

## Claim types enabled
- **Coordination** — partners/departments act on the same live schedule without logins.
- **Time saving** — the smart wizard auto-fills coverage gaps from inside the embed.
- **Visibility** — a filterable, real-time absence + coverage calendar on any site.
- **Consistency / brand** — embed is pixel- and behaviour-identical to the native app, fully localized (8 languages).

## Content angles
1. "Disqus-style embed, but for workforce scheduling": two-line `<effectime-embed>` snippet that ships the *entire* UI.
2. "Let your franchise partners fill their own shifts — with AI — without an Effectime login."
3. "Put a live, filterable team calendar on your intranet in 5 minutes" (how-to / SEO).
4. Before/after: stripped-down widget vs. full native parity (wizard modal + calendar) — demo GIF.
5. "Localized embeds": the same snippet renders correctly in HU/EN/DE/CS/SK/PL/RO.

## Funnel stage
- **Consideration** (developer/embed story differentiates vs. login-only competitors) and **retention/expansion** (existing customers extend Effectime into partner/intranet surfaces).
