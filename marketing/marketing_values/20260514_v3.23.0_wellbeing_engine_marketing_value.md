# v3.23.0 — Wellbeing scoring engine — Marketing Value

## One-sentence description

Per-member wellbeing scores (0–100) calculated from scheduling data —
overtime ratio, weekend density, leave utilization — with automatic
red/yellow/green alerts to managers when burnout risk crosses a threshold.

## Personas helped

- **CHRO / People Ops** — ESG-ready wellbeing reporting, automatic
  alerts when an employee is approaching burnout.
- **Workspace owners** — green/yellow/red distribution at a glance,
  Recalculate button for on-demand score refresh.
- **Compliance** — auditable wellbeing trail (immutable
  `wellbeing_scores` snapshots) for ESG and labor-law reporting.

## Claim types enabled

- **ESG-ready** — scheduling data is more predictive of burnout than
  surveys (Gallup). Effectime's scoring engine turns existing data
  into a Wellbeing Score without additional data collection.
- **Tier upsell driver** — Pro + Enterprise only; engagement-feature
  marketing screenshots cleanly justify the tier difference.
- **Competitive moat** — direct alternative to Peakon / Culture Amp
  (€8–15/employee/month standalone tools), bundled into the
  scheduling platform with the unfair advantage of richer data.
