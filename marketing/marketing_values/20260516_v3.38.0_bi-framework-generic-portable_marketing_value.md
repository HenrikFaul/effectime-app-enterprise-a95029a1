## Problem Solved (User Language)

"We bought another analytics tool and it gave us pretty charts but no structure for thinking about what the numbers actually mean. Every analyst on the team interprets the same metric differently. We have no way to ensure our BI outputs are trustworthy."

"We keep re-doing the same analysis from scratch every quarter. There's no institutional memory, no standards, and every new hire has to figure out our metrics from first principles."

"We want to use AI to help with data analysis but every time we ask a question, it just gives us a plausible-sounding answer with no source citations, no confidence level, and no mention of whether the data is even reliable."

---

## Personas That Benefit

- **Data-savvy founders and CEOs** who want their AI assistant to reason rigorously about business metrics, not just describe them.
- **Head of Analytics / BI Lead** who needs a standards framework that works across analysts and tools.
- **Product and operations teams** who want to self-serve BI analysis without a full data team.
- **Consultants and agencies** who work across multiple client projects and want a portable, reusable analytical framework.
- **Any team using LLMs for BI** who has experienced hallucinated metric definitions, fabricated benchmarks, or causation claims without evidence.

---

## Marketing Claims This Enables

- "Drop a single folder into any project. Instantly give your AI assistant rigorous, structured BI reasoning — metric definition, variance explanation, trend analysis, anomaly detection, forecasting, and board-ready narrative."
- "Every analysis is version-aware — the AI checks whether a software change caused the metric shift before attributing it to a business cause."
- "21 specialist prompt files cover the full BI lifecycle: strategy → KPI analysis → trend → anomaly → executive narrative → predictive signals → forecasting → governance."
- "Built-in anti-hallucination protocols prevent the AI from stating metric values without source citations, claiming causation without evidence, or using unverified baselines."
- "Works with any LLM — Claude, GPT-4, Gemini. Plain Markdown. No API dependencies."
- "Complete in 10 minutes: fill in the Project Context block and you are ready for a disciplined BI session."
