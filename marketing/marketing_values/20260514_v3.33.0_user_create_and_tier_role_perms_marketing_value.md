# v3.33.0 — Direct user creation + tier-filtered role permissions — Marketing Value

## One-sentence description
Admins can now create workspace members in two ways: invite by email
(self-service password) OR create directly with an admin-set password
(no email) — and role permissions only show what's actually in the
workspace's subscription tier.

## Personas
- **HR admins** — bulk-onboard teams without waiting for each member
  to open an email link.
- **Customer success / sales engineers** — provision pilot workspaces
  with full team data on demo day.
- **Workspace owners** — assign role permissions without seeing a wall
  of locked options they can't use.

## Claim types enabled
- **Frictionless onboarding** — direct creation is the difference
  between a manager deploying Effectime to 30 frontline workers in 5
  minutes vs 30 invite-email round-trips spread over a week.
- **Defense in depth** — password policy enforced both client-side
  (UX) and server-side (security), with the same source-of-truth
  Postgres function.
- **Tier-honest UX** — permissions only show what the customer paid
  for. No fake choices, no surprise locked screens.

## Content angles
1. **Customer Success enablement** — One-pager: "How to provision 30
   workers in 5 minutes" using the new create-user flow.
2. **Sales demo script** — End the pitch with: "Now let's create your
   team. No emails, no waiting." Live-create 5 users in 30 seconds.
3. **Internal audit doc** — Password policy explainer for compliance
   review (DSGVO works council, SOC 2 evidence).
