# v3.32.0 — Mobile PWA + Offline scaffold — Marketing Value

## One-sentence description
Effectime is now installable as a native-feeling app on iOS, Android,
and desktop — works offline, launches from the home screen, and
respects the connectivity reality of frontline workers (factory,
retail, healthcare, logistics).

## Personas
- **Frontline workers** — clock in even when the facility WiFi is
  rebooting; check tomorrow's schedule on the commute.
- **Operations managers** — deploy Effectime to 2.7B deskless workers
  per Emergence Capital's TAM number.
- **IT admins** — no app-store approval required; standard web app
  becomes installable.

## Claim types enabled
- **Deskless TAM** — per the strategy doc, WFM tools targeting
  deskless workers trade at 15-20× ARR vs. 5-8× for desktop-first tools.
- **No app store gatekeeping** — instant deploy, no Apple/Google review.
- **Cross-platform** — single codebase works as PWA + Capacitor native
  shell (iOS/Android already in package.json).

## Content angles
1. **"Install Effectime on your phone" landing page** — show the
   InstallPwaPrompt UX with a fake-screenshot mockup.
2. **Frontline-segment outreach** — Healthcare staffing, restaurant
   chains, retail HQ ops, logistics dispatchers.
3. **Reliability story** — "Works when your factory's WiFi doesn't."
