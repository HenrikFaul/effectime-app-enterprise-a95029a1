## 2. User-Language Description

Effectime's AI intelligence layer now covers every domain of workforce management analytics with specialist depth. Financial cost analysis, compliance audit readiness, workforce capacity planning, platform health monitoring, leading indicator detection, and board-level narrative generation are each governed by a dedicated reasoning system — not a generic analytics prompt. The BI system also includes formal governance standards for metric consistency and output quality, ensuring that every analysis produced is traceable, version-stamped, and confidence-rated.

---

## 3. Problem It Replaces

**Previously**: Domain-specific BI questions (labor cost variance, WTD compliance risk, workforce capacity gaps, churn signals) had no structured analytical protocol — analysis quality depended on the skill of the individual analyst or the quality of the ad-hoc prompt.

**Now**: Seven domain-specialist prompts with explicit protocols, metric catalogs, and output contracts ensure consistent, rigorous, decision-ready BI outputs across every key Effectime business domain.

---

## 4. Primary Beneficiary Personas

**Primary**: Effectime internal — AI agents producing BI outputs for product analytics, customer success, and engineering teams.

**Secondary (downstream value)**:
- **Operations managers and HR directors** who receive compliance, financial, and workforce capacity analyses and need to trust the rigor behind them.
- **Boards and investors** who receive formal board reports structured around Effectime's governance-grade board report template.
- **Customer success** who use platform health analytics to identify at-risk workspaces before churn occurs.

## 6. Claim Types Enabled

- **Compliance depth**: Compliance analytics with WTD-specific analysis, 7-dimension audit readiness scoring, and violation pattern detection.
- **Financial control**: Labor cost decomposition into volume/rate/efficiency drivers — not just "costs went up."
- **Workforce intelligence**: Headcount sufficiency modeling, capacity gap quantification, and turnover impact modeling built into the BI layer.
- **Risk transparency**: Predictive signal framework that surfaces leading indicators before lagging metrics confirm the problem.
- **Governance grade**: Formal metric lifecycle management, 3-tier BI output quality gates, and conflict resolution protocol — the infrastructure of enterprise analytics.
- **Executive communication**: Board report template and narrative storytelling framework that turn analysis into board-ready decisions, not data dumps.

## 7. Content Angles

1. **"The compliance score that means nothing"** — Article/LinkedIn on why a high compliance score without rule coverage analysis is false assurance. Use the audit readiness 7-dimension scorecard as the concrete framework.

2. **"Where does your labor cost actually go?"** — Content piece on cost variance decomposition (volume vs. rate vs. efficiency drivers). Position Effectime as the platform that surfaces the real driver, not just the total.

3. **"How to know your workspace is about to churn — 6 weeks in advance"** — Educational post on the 5-signal churn detection model. Platform health prompt as the underlying framework.

4. **"The data doesn't tell a story. You have to."** — Thought leadership on the difference between data and decision-making. Use the 5-part BI story arc (Situation/Complication/Question/Answer/Evidence) as a practical framework that any HR leader can use.

5. **"What a workforce planning model actually needs"** — Technical-adjacent content on capacity analysis: effective capacity formula, coverage ratios, turnover impact modeling. Positions Effectime as analytically sophisticated.
