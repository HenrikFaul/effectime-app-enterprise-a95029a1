# v3.19.0 — Customer Success Platform — Marketing Value

## One-sentence description

In-app onboarding checklist + NPS surveys + per-workspace health score
— so every Effectime customer knows whether their team is set up for
success, and we know who needs help before they churn.

## Personas helped

- **New workspace admins** — visible 7-step setup guide replaces a
  blank dashboard. Click any unchecked item to jump straight to the
  setup page.
- **Workspace owners** — see if your team has invited members,
  configured sites, published their first schedule, and installed the
  mobile app. Health score quantifies it.
- **Effectime Customer Success team** — health-score data + NPS
  responses let them prioritize outreach to at-risk workspaces.

## Claim types enabled

- **Activation** — onboarding completion correlates with 4× LTV
  (Intercom benchmark). The checklist makes completion visible and
  actionable.
- **Retention** — proactive NPS catches detractors early; aggregated
  health score flags at-risk workspaces.
- **Tier-agnostic** — even Freemium workspaces get the full
  customer-success layer. Conversion from Freemium → Pro is driven by
  successful onboarding, not by gating onboarding behind a paywall.

1. **Blog post** — "How Effectime turns onboarding into a churn-resistance
