# Marketing Value: Embed SDK — iframe Capacity Planner (v3.44.0)

## Problem solved (user language)
"Our client has their own CRM / operations portal. They want to see our capacity plan inside their system — not in a separate tab, not by logging into Effectime. They want it native."

Until now, every manager had to open Effectime separately to check staffing coverage. With the Embed SDK, the capacity planner appears live inside their existing tool, zero friction.

## Personas who benefit
- **Enterprise operations manager** — sees real-time capacity inside the CRM they already live in.
- **Optician / multi-site retail chain** — embeds coverage grid into franchise management portal without IT project.
- **IT department of enterprise client** — gets a two-line HTML snippet; no Effectime credentials needed in their system.
- **Effectime workspace admin** — self-service token management; no vendor involvement needed.

## Marketing claims enabled
- "Embed Effectime into any CRM or portal — one iframe snippet, no coding."
- "Real-time capacity visibility, wherever your team already works."
- "Token-based, revocable embed access — enterprise-grade security, zero friction."
- "Live shift coverage data in your CRM, refreshed on every page load."
- "No Effectime login required for your CRM users — data stays on your servers."
