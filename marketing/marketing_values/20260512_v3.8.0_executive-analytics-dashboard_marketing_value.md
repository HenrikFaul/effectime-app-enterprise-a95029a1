# Marketing Value — v3.8.0 Executive Intelligence Dashboard

## Feature name and version
Real-time Executive Intelligence Dashboard with Predictive Analytics — v3.8.0

## One-sentence user-language description
Effectime now gives HR managers and executives a live analytics command center: scheduled hours, labor costs, absence patterns, and coverage risk — all in one view, updated in real time.

## Problem it replaces
Manual Excel exports, ad-hoc headcount calculations, and end-of-month surprises when managers discover they've overspent on labor or left a team under-covered. The previous process required pulling data from multiple reports and assembling it by hand.

## Primary beneficiary personas
- **HR Manager / HR Director** — real-time labor cost and absence KPIs replace weekly manual reports
- **Operations Manager** — 13-week coverage risk heatmap eliminates scheduling blind spots
- **Owner / CEO** — 6-month cost forecast enables budget planning without spreadsheets

## Marketing-ready claim
"See exactly what your workforce costs, who's at risk of absence, and where you'll be short-staffed — weeks before it happens."

## Claim types enabled
- **Visibility** — replaces manual report assembly with live KPIs
- **Cost reduction** — labor cost MTD + 6-month forecast enables proactive budget management
- **Time saving** — no more manual Excel exports for leadership reviews
- **Risk prevention** — coverage risk heatmap surfaces problems 13 weeks ahead
