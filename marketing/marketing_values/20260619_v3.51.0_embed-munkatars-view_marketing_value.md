# Marketing Value — v3.51.0 Embed "Munkatárs" per-person schedule + workload (and a leaner tab set)

**Feature name / version:** Embedded per-employee schedule & workload view ("Munkatárs") — v3.51.0

## One-sentence user-language description
On any embedded Effectime calendar you can now pick any team member from a dropdown and instantly see their whole month — where they work each day, their days off, and whether they're under- or over-booked — and assign them with one click.

## Problem it replaces
The embed's per-person view used to be a confusing two-step "pick someone, then look at one week" with no sense of workload, and a separate office-headcount tab duplicated what the capacity view already showed. Managers couldn't quickly answer "is this person over-scheduled this month?" without leaving the embed.

## Primary beneficiary persona(s)
- **Ops / team lead** — checks and balances an individual's monthly load at a glance.
- **HR manager** — sees one employee's schedule + absences together, on a shared page.
- **Franchise / partner viewer** — looks up a specific person's coverage without an Effectime login.

## Marketing-ready claim
"See any employee's full month — shifts, days off, and over/under-booking — in one glance, right inside an embedded calendar, and reschedule them with a click."

## Claim types enabled
- **Visibility** — per-person month + workload at a glance.
- **Coordination** — assign/adjust one person directly in the embed.
- **Time saving** — no drilling through multiple screens; inline person switcher.
- **Simplicity / UX** — leaner, clearer tab set (removed a redundant view).

## Content angles
