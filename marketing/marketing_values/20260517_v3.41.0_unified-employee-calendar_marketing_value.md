# Marketing Value — v3.41.0 Unified Employee Calendar + Claim Fix

**Date:** 2026-05-17  
**Version:** 3.41.0  
**Feature slug:** unified-employee-calendar

## Problem solved (user language)

> "I don't know where I'm working this week."  
> "I have to go to two different places just to mark my availability and check my schedule."  
> "When I try to claim an open shift, I just get an error with no explanation."

## Personas that benefit

- **Deskless employee** (retail, optician, field worker): needs to know their schedule at a glance from their phone; doesn't understand why the app shows availability in one tab and hours in another
- **Part-time / variable-hours worker**: marks preferred days → manager assigns shifts → wants immediate visual confirmation in the same screen
- **Manager / owner**: employees who can't see their own schedule generate unnecessary "where am I working?" calls

## What changed

1. The "Saját idő" (time-attendance) calendar now doubles as the availability calendar. One place for everything.
2. A new "My upcoming schedule" card in the portal immediately shows the next 14 days of office assignments.
3. Open-shift claiming now works correctly and shows specific, helpful error messages when it can't.

## Marketing claims enabled

- "One calendar — mark availability, see assignments, track hours"
- "Employees always know where they work — no separate screens, no confusion"
- "Open shift claiming that actually works: instant confirmation or a clear reason why not"
- "Built for deskless workers: everything on one mobile screen"

## Content angles

- **Before/after UX**: show the old two-card layout vs. the merged single calendar
- **Mobile-first story**: the unified view is especially powerful on small screens
- **"No surprises" reliability**: fixing the claim bug is a trust story — the app does what it promises
- **Data flow explainer**: availability → assignment → timesheet → payroll; all in one calendar
