---

## User-language problem solved

Before this release, the Employee Self-Service dashboard felt cluttered:
- Past open shifts appeared alongside current ones — confusing and irrelevant
- "Nyitott műszakok" (Open Shifts) lived in a separate card, disconnected from the Shift Marketplace where it logically belongs
- The clock-in stamp and attendance stats occupied two large separate sections, wasting screen space on mobile and desktop alike

After: the dashboard is cleaner, more logical, and more space-efficient — everything related to shifts is in one place, and the clock-in interaction fits neatly beside the stats without dominating the page.

---

## Personas that benefit

- **Deskless / frontline worker (retail, factory, healthcare):** The most-used actions (clock in/out, check open shifts) are visually grouped and compact — less scrolling, faster task completion on a phone.
- **Office employee on self-service:** Attendance stats and clock-in live side-by-side — one glance covers both.
- **Shift coordinator reviewing their own availability:** Open shift requests are directly inside the Shift Marketplace card, making the shift trading and claiming workflow coherent in a single card.
