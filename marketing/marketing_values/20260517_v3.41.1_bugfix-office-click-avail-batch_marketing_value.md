# Marketing Value — v3.41.1 Bug fixes

**Date:** 2026-05-17  
**Version:** 3.41.1  
**Feature slug:** bugfix-office-click-avail-batch

## Problem solved (user language)

> "When I click the office name in the capacity planner, the whole screen goes white."  
> "I can see the green availability indicator but I can't click on it to change it."  
> "In the batch fill dialog I can't set the site/telephely — I have to do it day by day."

## Personas that benefit

- **Manager / owner**: can now navigate from the capacity planner directly to an office's settings without a crash
- **Deskless employee**: can now mark their availability on any day — including days they've already clocked hours
- **Employee on variable schedule**: can assign a work site to a full week in one batch operation instead of editing each day individually

## What changed

1. Clicking an office name in the capacity planner now correctly navigates to that office's settings row and highlights it — no more white screen.
2. Employees can tap any calendar day to cycle their availability (available → preferred → unavailable → clear), even on days with recorded time segments.
3. The batch fill dialog now includes an "Office / Site" dropdown — selecting an office applies the site assignment to all days in the batch.

## Marketing claims enabled

- "The capacity planner is fully navigable — click any office to jump straight to its settings"
- "Availability is always editable — no hidden rules, no blocked days"
- "Set your work site for an entire week in one step with batch fill"

## Content angles

- **Reliability story**: fixes that eliminate white screens and non-functional UI = trust in the product
- **Productivity**: batch office assignment saves repeated taps for employees on fixed-site weeks
