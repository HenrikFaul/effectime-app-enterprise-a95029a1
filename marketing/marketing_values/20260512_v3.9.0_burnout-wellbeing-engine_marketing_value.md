# Marketing Value — v3.9.0 Burnout & Wellbeing Detection Engine

## Feature name and version
Predictive Burnout & Wellbeing Detection Engine — v3.9.0

## One-sentence user-language description
Effectime now automatically calculates a wellbeing score for every team member and alerts managers before burnout becomes a resignation.

## Problem it replaces
Burnout is invisible until it's too late — managers notice only when someone quits or takes extended sick leave. There was no system-level signal to act on. Wellbeing "check-ins" were informal and inconsistent.

## Primary beneficiary personas
- **HR Manager** — automated alerts replace guesswork about who is at risk
- **Team Lead / Operations Manager** — heatmap view gives a team-wide wellbeing snapshot at a glance
- **Employee** — self-view score in My Portal enables proactive self-care with system-backed leave hints

## Marketing-ready claim
"Effectime detects burnout risk before it costs you a resignation — with a data-driven wellbeing score for every team member."

## Claim types enabled
- **Risk prevention** — early warning before burnout reaches resignation stage
- **Retention** — proactive wellbeing support reduces unwanted turnover
- **Visibility** — managers see team-wide wellbeing, not just individual anecdotes
- **Compliance** — documented wellbeing monitoring supports employer duty-of-care obligations
