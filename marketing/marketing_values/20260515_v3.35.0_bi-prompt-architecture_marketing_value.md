## 1. Feature Name and Version

Business Intelligence Prompt Architecture — v3.35.0

---

## 2. User-Language Description

Effectime now has a structured, repository-aware intelligence layer that makes it possible to interpret the platform's workforce metrics with the same rigor as a senior business analyst — without requiring one on staff. When a manager or founder asks why compliance scores dropped, why overtime spiked, or whether a wellbeing trend is real, the system can locate the correct data source, check for software-related explanations, and produce a structured, decision-ready answer with an explicit confidence rating.

---

## 3. Problem It Replaces

**Previously**: BI interpretation in Effectime relied on human analysts or ad-hoc AI prompting without a structured framework — leading to metric misinterpretations, ignored version history, missing data quality caveats, and inconsistent reporting formats.

**Now**: A governed BI reasoning system with anti-hallucination rules, mandatory evidence-grounding protocols, version-aware analysis, and standardized output contracts produces trustworthy, decision-oriented BI outputs consistently.

---

## 4. Primary Beneficiary Personas

**Primary**: Effectime internal — AI agents producing BI outputs, product and analytics teams building reporting capabilities.

**Secondary (downstream value)**:
- **HR leaders and operations managers** who receive BI outputs and need to trust the analysis they act on.
- **Founders** who need board-ready summaries grounded in real data, not dashboard screenshots.
- **Compliance officers** who require audit-trail-linked metric interpretations, not bare numbers.

---

## 5. Marketing-Ready Claim

"Effectime doesn't just collect workforce data — it interprets it. Every KPI is grounded in source-verified definitions, every trend is checked against version history, and every executive summary is produced with an explicit confidence rating. This is business intelligence that tells you what it doesn't know as clearly as what it does."

---

## 6. Claim Types Enabled

- **Reliability**: BI outputs are version-stamped and evidence-grounded — not black-box AI assertions.
- **Auditability**: Every metric cited in a BI output can be traced to its source table, migration, and computation formula.
- **Decision support**: The framework is designed to produce decision-ready outputs, not data dumps.
- **Risk transparency**: Explicit data quality scoring and confidence ratings distinguish "we know" from "we estimate."
- **Operational intelligence**: The framework covers the full BI lifecycle — from metric definition through anomaly detection through executive narrative.
