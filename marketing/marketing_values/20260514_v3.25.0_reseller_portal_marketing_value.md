# v3.25.0 — Reseller portal — Marketing Value

## One-sentence description
B2B2B platform layer: HR consultancies, payroll bureaus, and IT MSPs
can provision enterprise workspaces under their own brand and collect a
revenue share — a 30-70% reduction in CAC for the channel.

## Personas
- **HR consultancy partners** — sell Effectime under their own brand.
- **Effectime sales** — channel-led growth without direct enterprise sales.
- **Platform admins** — onboard new resellers, configure revenue share.

## Claim types enabled
- **Strategic premium (platform vs. app)** — per Rippling's partner program report (35% of new ARR via channel in 2024).
- **TAM expansion** — 200+ DACH/CEE HR consultancies in the addressable channel network.
- **Tier upsell driver** — reseller is Enterprise-tier-only; clear value tier.
