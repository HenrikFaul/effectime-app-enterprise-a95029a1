## User-language problem solved

Czech, Slovak, Polish, German, Austrian, Romanian, and English managers were seeing Hungarian weekday names ("H", "K", "Sze", "Cs", "P") and month names ("január", "február") in calendars, date pickers, and schedule views — even when the app was set to their own language. This created a confusing, unprofessional user experience that undermined trust for non-Hungarian customers.
