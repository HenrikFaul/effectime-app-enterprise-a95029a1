# v3.17.0 — Workspace tier persistence + visibility — Marketing Value

## Feature name and version

Tier transparency for every workspace + audited platform-admin tier
change (v3.17.0).

## One-sentence user-language description

Every workspace now wears its subscription tier as a visible badge on
the dashboard, picker, and admin panel — and only a platform admin can
change it, with a permanent audit log entry every time.

## Problem it replaces

A paying customer's confidence depends on being able to verify, at any
moment, that they're getting what they paid for. Before v3.17.0:

- The tier of a workspace was invisible after creation.
- Feature visibility came from a 5-minute-cached merge of tier + addons
  + overrides, so the available feature set could appear to "shift"
  without the tier itself changing.
- There was no admin UI to change a workspace tier after creation —
  short of recreating the workspace, a Freemium-to-Pro upgrade was
  impossible.

The user described it as "random tier changes" and rightly flagged it as
a billing-and-trust-critical bug.

## Primary beneficiary personas

- **Paying customers (workspace owners)** — can verify their tier at a
  glance every day. No more uncertainty about "what am I paying for?"
- **Customer Success / Sales engineering** — can upgrade a customer's
  tier in a single click after a closed sales call, with a permanent
  audit trail for billing reconciliation.
- **Platform admins** — see every workspace's tier in a single table
  column, can audit the tier distribution at a glance, and have one
  enforced way (with audit log) to make a change.
- **Compliance / billing** — every tier change has a permanent
  `platform_audit_events` row with actor, timestamp, prev/new, and
  reason. Audit-ready.

## Marketing-ready claim

"Subscription clarity, not subscription mystery. Every Effectime
workspace shows its tier on every screen, and changing it is a one-click
audited operation by your platform team — no support tickets, no
unexplained feature shifts."

## Claim types enabled

- **Trust / transparency** — paying customers verify their entitlement
  directly in the UI. No more "wait, do I have Pro features today?"
- **Audit & compliance** — every tier change has actor, reason,
  timestamp, prev_state, new_state in an immutable audit table.
- **Operational efficiency** — Customer Success can finalize an
  upgrade in 5 seconds instead of escalating an engineering ticket.
