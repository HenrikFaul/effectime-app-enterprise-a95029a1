## 4. Authority-Building Roadmap

### What to Publish First and Why

**Month 1 priority: Shift Scheduling Pillar** — because:
- Műszakbeosztás is the core product feature and the most specific, least competitive HU-language search topic
- Summer publishing window (June) coincides with peak seasonal pain
- Creates the internal link foundation that every other pillar references
- Demonstrates topical depth to Google before broader HR category content is published

**Rule:** Never publish a broad topic (HR szoftver) before you have deep authority on the narrow topic (műszakbeosztás). Google rewards topical depth before topical breadth.
