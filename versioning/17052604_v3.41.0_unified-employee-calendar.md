## Summary

Three related improvements that complete the employee self-service experience:

1. **`claim_open_shift` bug fixed** — the `ON CONFLICT DO NOTHING` on `enterprise_shift_assignments` silently swallowed double-booking errors instead of raising an explicit exception, causing a generic "try again" toast. Fixed by adding an explicit `already_assigned` guard before the INSERT. Five specific error codes now have user-friendly messages.

2. **AvailabilityCalendar merged into EmployeeMonthView** — employees now mark their availability (tap to cycle available → preferred → unavailable) directly in the time-attendance calendar, alongside their recorded hours and site assignment. This removes the duplicate Elérhetőség card from the self-service portal and creates the single source of truth the user requested: availability → manager assigns shifts → employee sees both in one view → data flows to payroll.

3. **Upcoming schedule card** — a new card in the self-service portal shows the next 14 days of office assignments, answering the most-requested question: "which office am I working at today/this week?"
