## Implemented boundary

- `BusinessRoleManager` edits a bounded, canonical full allocation snapshot and
  saves through the existing optimistic atomic member-profile RPC.
- Tenant-wide role deletion is one locked server transaction with fresh tenant,
  RBAC and entitlement checks, deterministic rebalance, exact postconditions and
  a data-minimised audit receipt.
- Instant-identity creation records a durable provisioning intent before Auth
  creation. Four saga states and six service-role-only RPCs bind the exact
  tenant-owned user, prepare DB compensation before Auth deletion, reject
  profile/membership resurrection and finalize only after exact postconditions.
- `create-instant-enterprise-member`, `join-event` and the then-current cleanup
  worker share the same strict saga contract. Web, Android and iOS retain one
  React/Supabase data plane and no native privileged key.

## Compatibility, rollout and rollback

The two migrations are forward-only and intentionally unapplied. Rollout is
database/schema-cache first, then all matching Edge writers/workers, then the
same attestable web/Android/iOS client. A pending saga or active cleanup lease
must never be deleted as rollback. Production remains **NO-GO** until migration
history and clean replay are reconciled, restored-staging fault injection is
green, recurring cleanup is installed and monitored, and live SHA/artifact
attestation identifies the deployed release.
